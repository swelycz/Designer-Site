Theresa Prestel

Interior Designer

Principle designer and owner, Theresa Prestel founded Prestel Interiors, in 2001, a full service interior design firm. Theresa is a graduate of Columbus College of Art & Design where she received a Bachelor of Fine Arts degree in Interior Design. While establishing Prestel Interiors, Theresa worked as an Interior Designer for Thomasville Furnishings. This is where Theresa met many of her client’s and was able to demonstrate her passion and skill for interior design. As a result, Prestel Interior’s has had great opportunity to provide interior design services to some of the most prestigious home builders; Including Romanelli & Hughes, Jimenez-Haid Builders and Kevin Knight in Central Ohio. Prestel Interiors is a member of ASID, American Society of Interior Designers, as well as a member of the National Kitchen and Bath Association.
